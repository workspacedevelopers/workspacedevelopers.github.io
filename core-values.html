Our Vision

As the largest technical student body at University of Kerala, We aspire to be the pioneer in upskilling students through practical learning. Throughout the program students build project that solve 1 of the 17 United Nations  Sustainable Development  Goals

Our vision aligns with shifts in the educational, and technological landscape, which animates our mission and our values for preparing students to succeed in  the changing world

If you love culture, and want to understand better how technology works at its core, then Workspace might just be the perfect place for you. You'll get to learn from some of the best brains in the industry, all of that for absolutely no cost. You'll enjoy learning in the environment!

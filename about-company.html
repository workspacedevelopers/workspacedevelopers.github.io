OUR HISTORY

We are More than Just
Another Student Community

Workspace founded in 2021, has established itself as a paradigm of education and entrepreneurship among students and premier institutes in the city. Learning alongside those who are currently doing well in the field can be a wonderful way to accelerate your own learning curve. Consequently, the student program is designed to give the students a taste of the real world, with exclusive access to expert training, mentorship, and networking opportunities that can help nurture their problem-solving & leadership  skill driven by the will to build for a billion users

With a goal to empower the developer community, Workspace for Education focuses on doing our bit to prepare students to create the future. By tutoring them to excel in a diverse skill set while learning in a peer-to-peer environment and maintaining a competitive  advantage  in the changing  technological landscape

Workspace is a gateway for budding entrepreneurs who want to take their first step in the world of startups and need guidance with technology, product, and leadership while they're learning how to lead their team. Workspace for Startup aims to scout exceptional talent into the world of entrepreneurship by providing tailored training, and helping them  transform themselves into  businesses

Our Commitment to Work with People with Disabilities

Workspaceâs disability policy outlines our provisions for people with disabilities. We want to make our services, and products available to all people respecting their individual needs, pride, freedom, equal opportunity, and make full use of our facilities. Disabilities that this policy refers to include but are not limited to conditions that require the use of a wheelchair or other movement support device or mechanism, physical impairments that obstruct everyday activities, visual, hearing, speech, or mental impediment, conditions that require assistance or constant medical care. Our Commitment Against Substance Abuse and Drugs

Alcohol and drug misuse pose a threat to the health and safety of Workspace members and to the security of the community's facilities. For these reasons, Workspace is committed to the elimination of drug and alcohol use and misuse. This applies to all of our members. Workspace will assist and support members who voluntarily seek help for drug or alcohol addiction before becoming subject to discipline or termination under this or other Workspace policies. Such members will be allowed time off, placed on leaves of absence, referred to treatment providers, and otherwise accommodated as required by law. Members may be required to document that they are successfully following prescribed treatment and to take and pass follow-up tests if they hold roles that are safety-sensitive, or require driving, or if they have violated this policy previously

Your Data Security and Privacy is at Top of Our Priority

Any information that a member learns about Workspace, its members, or donors, as a result of working for Workspace that is not otherwise publicly available constitutes confidential information. Members may not disclose confidential information to anyone who is not associated with Workspace or to other persons associated with the coreteam who do not need to know such information to assist rendering services. The protection of privileged and confidential information, including secrets, is vital to the interests and the success of Workspace. The disclosure, distribution, electronic transmission or copying of Workspaceâs confidential information is prohibited. Such information includes, but is not limited to the programs, projects, and/or the financial information.
